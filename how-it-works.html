How SugoSure Works

SugoSure connects patients with their doctor and health coach through ConnectedHealth's cloud-based Clinical Data Platform.

This transforms how care is delivered, enabling doctor and health coach to set Clinical and Lifestyle Plans then supporting patients to achieve their goals and improve diabetic control.

Key Components

Patient App

Feedback, support and guidance to inform and motivate your self-management.

Physician Portal

Closer monitoring and earlier problem resolution by your doctor.

Health Coach Portal

Your own health coach to help you focus on what's important and work with you to achieve your lifestyle goals.
