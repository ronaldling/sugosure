DON'T LEAVE IT UNTIL IT'S TOO LATE​

Not getting your diabetes control right can be serious

Sub-optimal or poor blood glucose control over several years significantly increases your risk of diabetic complications such as heart disease, stroke, eye and kidney problems. In Singapore, diabetes is still the dominant cause of end stage kidney failure.

Getting it right means following your doctor's Clinical Plan and actively managing your lifestyle

Just taking your medicines is not enough to achieve good blood glucose control. Actively managing your lifestyle is also critical: the right combination of medications and lifestyle changes can result in much lower risk of complications in the longer term.

The good news is that complications can be avoided through an appropriate Care Plan developed by your doctor and good self-management.
